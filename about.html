About Us

Nepal is florished with natural beauties. If you want to be in Zen with nature with the rivers, lakes, mountains, forests etc which are jewel of our country which have made its identity unique in the world. Putting that context those natural beauties, in our mind we five young youths have decided to start a small company Z Star Homes. Our vision of home stay in Mustang and Mugu has come to life. Our company is operated and promoted by ourselves and has enrolled professional guides who are responsible for providing a wide range of culture, heritage and tours as well as other extra activities that are connected with himalayas destinations. Beside this, for being legalized, we have planned to get registration with Ministry of Tourism, Tourism Board, Office of Cottage and Small Industry. Similarly, we have also planned to co-operate with Tax Office of Nepal and all tourism activities to display our company as civilized organization. Last but not least, we promise to create a safe and sacred environment and entertain our guests by providing typical Nepali food items with suitable accommodation and cultural experience.

ZstarHomes is our beautiful safe heaven for travellers around the world. Everybody is welcome over here. Our first priority is to serve the visitors.This company was established in 2012 A.D.for helping tourist all around the world.We started this company small idea of providing some local authentic experience to the people all around the world and make our culture known all over the world.

So what we provide is a gate way to a life time experience of primitive life style of Mustang and Jumla which are the remote part of Nepal. These people have to go through harsh forces of nature and continue their daily lives. Which is kind of cool thing right? This homestay is ment for travellers who want to travel to exotic places of the world and enjoy exhilarating and thrilling experience of this landscape and make this their lasting memory to cherish for.
