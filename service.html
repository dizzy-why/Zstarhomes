Information about our Homestay

We would like to share with you a few tips on what you are to expect while staying in Homestay, and we will make a few little suggestions that are required and helpful for you as a Homestay Guest.

  • Homestay offers breakfast and dinner everyday as your choice typically local dishes.
  • You will have your own furnished bedroom, a bed, desk, adequate lighting, warmth provided in winter, cooling in summer and access to a bathroom and space to hang your clothes and store your belongings.
  • Bed linen, sheets, quilt cover and towel will be provided.
  • You will have access to the kitchen and living room allowing you to sit with the family,watch television, and share in families activities.
  • Each guests will have their own room, or you can share a room in a group as per your wish.
  • You will be given a key to the home on arrival, so you are able to gain access to the Homestay.
  • Your Homestay includes the cost of only dishes and local wines you choose and the cultural performance you desire to watch.
  • You will not be charged extra for water, electricity,fire,no additional charge for the use of the Internet etc.
  • Tour packing of Upper mustang and Lower Mustang along with Rara Lake
  • On arrival at Homestay you will receive a Guest Orientation and Information Kit, which has been posted to you before your arrival.
  • Inside the Guest Orientation and Information Kit there will be the tourist Guidelines, brochures, maps and other information about Mustang to make the most of your stay easier and convienient.
